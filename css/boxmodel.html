KAZAMA

Kazama (Toru Kazama) is a main character in Crayon Shin-chan and Shin-chan's well-groomed, often stressed-out friend and classmate. He comes from a wealthy family with an architect father who works abroad and a strict, affluent mother who prioritizes his education. Kazama is known for losing his temper when Shin-chan teases him and often bickers with him.

NENEI

Nene Sakurada is a five-year-old girl in the anime Crayon Shin-chan, known for her bossy nature and love for playing "real house". Like her mother, she has a violent temper and expresses her anger by punching a stuffed rabbit. She often forces her friends, especially Masao, into unwanted roles during her games and sees herself as a rival to the popular Ai-chan.

BOOCHAN

Bochan, whose real name is Bo Suzuki, is a quiet, slow-talking, but intelligent member of Shin-chan's friend group in Crayon Shin-chan. He is known for his love of collecting stones, an interest in urban legends, and a peculiar trail of mucus from his nose, though he is often seen using it to perform tricks.
